Artists Reaching Out

A student club at Columbia University

We bring visual arts education to youth in the Morningside Heights and Harlem community.

About

Artists Reaching Out (ARO) is a student-run volunteer program at Columbia University that brings visual arts education to preschool through middle school students in the Morningside Heights and Harlem community.

As part of Community Impact at Columbia, our volunteers foster positive mentoring relationships and cultivate young talent. We seek to foster diversity and inclusion in the visual arts both inside and outside of the classroom.

Get Involved

No arts experience required. There is no need to be an artist or have teaching experience. Everyone is welcome.

Note: As of 2026, we require volunteers to have a Social Security Number (SSN) per NYC DOE.

To join, email us at aro@communityimpact.columbia.edu with your academic year, school affiliation, and a brief statement about why you're interested.
