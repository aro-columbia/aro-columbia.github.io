About

Artists Reaching Out (ARO) is a student-run volunteer program at Columbia University that brings visual arts education to preschool through middle school students in the Morningside Heights and Harlem community.

As part of Community Impact at Columbia, our volunteers foster positive mentoring relationships and cultivate young talent. We seek to foster diversity and inclusion in the visual arts both inside and outside of the classroom.
